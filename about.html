Our Story

Established in Nairobi, Kenya

Founded in 2020, Bean & Brew started as a humble coffee cart with a single mission: to source the finest ethical coffee beans and roast them with precision, passion, and care.

We work directly with local farmers to ensure fair trade practices and sustainable cultivation. Whether you are grabbing a quick morning espresso or settling in for an afternoon study session, our space is thoughtfully designed to be your cozy home away from home.

"Sourced Responsibly. Roasted Locally."
